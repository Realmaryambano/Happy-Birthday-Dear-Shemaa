Our Story 📖

A decade, told in scraps of memory. Scroll slow.

10+ years ago

I met her over ten years ago. My first thought? She was rude. And that was my biggest misconception about her GOD. I was a bully back then, and I loved nothing more than disturbing her on purpose.

soon after

Time passed, and somewhere along the way, disturbing her turned into loving being around her. Funny how that happens.

the jokes era

I loved making jokes with her. Oh my god, I'm still smiling and blushing right now, like she's my husband or boyfriend. Yeah, she wasn't less than any boyfriend to me.

under 13

We were kids, both under thirteen. We didn't know what love even meant. But we made sure to build memories that weigh more than today's so-called love.

school days

Oh my god, our teachers were jealous of our friendship. LITERALLY jealous. They made her sit away from me, like I was some kind of trouble. I'm not gonna lie though — hehe — I probably was.

family knew her name

Even my own family knew her. That's how important this human was to me. Even today, till date, my mom texts her

the long calls

I loved talking to her on calls before I even knew what a "long call" was. Before the trend of screenshotting call durations existed. Before trendy Pinterest-style photos were even a thing. It was just us, being kids, being close. We don't even have pictures together except 8 or 10.

a fair warning to the world 🔪💅

IF ANYONE EVER HURTS HER, OR EVEN BRINGS TEARS TO HER EYES — I won't do anything but curse you so badly that you may never find peace. Hahaha, seriously. Never get a good night's sleep, and may mosquitoes always bite you whenever you try to. May your pillow never give you rest. So be careful before hurting her, okayyyy? 😌
